Elizabeth Smith was indicted for a Misdemeanour for breakings the House of John Walters , in the day time, with intention to steal his Goods while a little Girl went out to fetch a pint of Drink, in the mean time the Prisoner came and opened the Door; but the Girl returning, she was for getting away, but the Girl laid hold of her, and cried out, and People coming, they took her, and found a pick-lock key in her Bosom, which lock'd and unlock'd the Door very well. It was fully proved, the Jury found her Guilty . [Fine. See summary.] [Whipping. See summary.]
