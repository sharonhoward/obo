Jane Wilson of the Parish of St. Andrews Holbourn , was Indicted for Felony and Robbery, for Robbing the House of Jeremy Deere , in the Day time, and putting Elizabeth his Wife in fear on the 18th of August last, and taking thence 2 Holland Shirts, 8 Smocks a Silver Fringe, and Divers other Goods . The Prosecutors Wife deposed that she sitting below Stairs in the Kitchen heard a great noise above, upon which she went into the Street and called some People to her assistance, who coming, found the Prisoner with her Lap full of Goods, which she had taken out of her Drawers. The Prisoner denied the Fact, saying she knew nothing of it: But it was fully proved against her. The Jury found her Guilty of Felony. [Branding. See summary.]
